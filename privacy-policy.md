# Privacy Policy for Demons Streak

Last Updated: June 2026

Demons Streak collects and stores only the information necessary to provide streak tracking features within Discord servers.

Data collected may include:

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server IDs
* Streak statistics and timestamps
* Role assignment data related to streak rewards

Demons Streak does not sell user data.

Message content is processed only as required for streak functionality and is not permanently stored unless necessary for bot operations.

Users may remove the bot from their server at any time.

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the bot developer.
